The Story of Drywall

Once upon a time, at Cofounders, a merry band of startup hackers and designers in Singapore, Drywall was invented to visualise software complexity.

The Cofounders team helped those in need of software product design & development using Post-it notes, Sharpie pens, Blu-Tack, and other stationary shop essentials.

Tired of digitising walls full of Post-it notes, Drywall was created as a project management tool for GitHub Issues.
